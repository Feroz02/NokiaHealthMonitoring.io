Project Abstract: Financial Health Monitoring for Nokia Corporation

This project focuses on the financial health monitoring and forecasting of Nokia Ltd., a global telecommunications and consumer electronics company, using data-driven approaches. This study leverages historical financial data of Nokia Ltd. and applies advanced time series forecasting and regression techniques to model and predict key financial indicators such as revenue and net profit.

This project also forecasts measures and steps the company could have taken to sustain in the mobile phone industry. Various financial data is also demonstrated to show the comparison with rival brands such as Apple and Samsung, and the possible outcome if Nokia Ltd had adopted the suggested changes and measures.

The project concludes with recommendations for Nokia’s financial sustainability and risk mitigation strategies based on forecast trends. And it also indicates that a successful company like NOKIA who were the leader in Handset Manufacturing can have a downfall when they fail to have a forecast of new technologies over the years which resulted in change of their stream to networking by which their competitors succeeded and resulted in loss for the Nokia Ltd as a mobile phone manufacturer.
